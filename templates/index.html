Enhancing Aviation Safety

Welcome to the future of aviation safety for single-prop pilots. Our initiative tackles fatigue—the hidden risk—by leveraging cutting-edge biometric data to improve reaction times and decision-making, ensuring safer skies.

Fatigue Monitoring System

Pilot fatigue is one of the most critical threats to aviation safety. Our innovative system uses real-time biometric analysis—monitoring heart rate, sleep patterns, and physical activity—to detect fatigue early and prevent incidents before they occur.

Biometric Metrics We Monitor

  • Heart Rate: Tracks beats per minute to identify stress and fatigue levels.
  • Blood Oxygen: Measures oxygen saturation in the blood to assess overall health.
  • Temperature: Monitors body temperature fluctuations to detect anomalies.
  • Blood Pressure (Systolic): Measures the pressure in arteries during heartbeats.
  • Blood Pressure (Diastolic): Tracks the pressure in arteries between beats.
  • Reaction Time: Captures response speed to gauge cognitive alertness.
  • Hours Slept: Records sleep duration to evaluate restfulness and fatigue risk.
  • Steps Walked: Counts daily steps to understand physical activity levels.
